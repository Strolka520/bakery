Meet Carol...

Carol is a Saginaw native and proud mother of four daughters. Cooking is her passion; with special notice to baking. She has several homemade recipes which have been passed down through her family for years.

Outside of the kitchen, Carol likes spending family time on the shores of Lake Superior or at her cabin in Standish. She is also an avid gardener. During the warmer months, Carol enjoys tending to her plot at the Saginaw Children's Zoo.

Her hope is that each of her customers loves her baked creations just as much as she does.
